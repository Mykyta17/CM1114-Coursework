What is Gymmaxing

Gymmaxing is a task aimed for boosting muscle mass of all the participants of the Looksmaxing Society
Every task is performed under the strict control of the Mentor so the risk of any injuries is extremely low
After a couple of questions and tests each participant will get his individual plan of training so they will be fully capable of passing the final exam.
