What is Mogging

Mogging is a term used to describe a battle of attractiveness between two people to decide who is the winner "Mogger" and who is the loser "Mogee"
In our Looksmaxing Society mogging battles have very strict rules so no participants will get harmed during them.
Outcome of the mogging battle does not have an impact on your rank in the Society but everyday practice can significantly boost your chances of passing the final exam.
